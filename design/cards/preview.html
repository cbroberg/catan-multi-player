Design Notes

Color Palette:

  • Background: #0e1a2e (dark navy)
  • Resource cards match their terrain hex colors
  • Dev cards use purple/magical theme (#4a1a6b to #2a0e40)
  • Accents: #ce93d8 (light purple), #ffd54f (gold)

Accessibility:

  • Each resource card has a unique colorblind pattern at bottom (stripes, cross-hatch, dots, lines, triangles)
  • Icons are distinct in shape, not just color
  • Text labels on every card
  • Building costs uses checkmark/lock icons, not just color for affordable state

Typography: Georgia serif throughout for medieval/premium feel

Sizing: Resource cards 50x70px, Dev cards 60x80px, all panels fit 375px mobile width
